To view the sample program, open the WindowsFormApplication1 folder and then open the WindowsFormsApplication1.sln file.

Each form corresponds to the steps in the notes as shown in the parentheses next to the form name.

To change the form that is shown, open the Program.cs file.
Go to line 18 and change Form1 to the form that you want (Form2, Form3, Form4...)(do not type the parentheses).
